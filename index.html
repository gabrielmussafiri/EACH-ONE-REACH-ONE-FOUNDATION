ABOUT US & MISSION

If we are Helping Each One Reach One then the world will be always happy.

Each One Reach One began its journey in 2022 when Mr. Thato Shale recognized the need for creating opportunities for the community. Inspired by his missionary work and driven by the desire to uplift others, he joined forces with Mrs. Innocentia Maluleke and Mr. Khongelani Maluleke. Together, they laid the foundation for the organization and embarked on a mission to provide skills development and support to individuals seeking personal and professional growth.


At Each One Reach One, our mission is to empower individuals and communities by providing them with opportunities for skill development and personal growth. We aim to bridge the gap between talent and resources by collaborating with sponsors and partners. Through initiatives such as our partnership with Moteo Construction, we offer construction internships to talented young individuals, equipping them with the necessary skills and training to excel in their chosen field. We strive to continue expanding our impact through community involvement and the support of generous donations from individuals and corporations. .
